Injectable Forms of Estradiol

Estradiol valerate

Typically found under names such as Progynon Depot and Delestrogen, this form of injectable estradiol is easily used for estradiol monotherapy and it has a long half-life, so the user would not have to use the medication as often.

Typical dosages are 4mg/5-7 days in the case of monotherapy, which is the recommended method when injecting estradiol.
