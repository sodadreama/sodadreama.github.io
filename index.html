Gray's Endocrinology

Welcome! This is a surface-level guide to the pharmacological aspect of transitioning, whether it be case studies, the common uses for various medications, some basic anatomy involved with these regimens, or some edge-case medications that may be useful, this is where to look!

The information on this website usually comes from my notes on various hormone regimens. If I have missed anything or there is some new information out there, contact me on discord at fishinthepool#6461

If you are just starting (DIY) HRT, your best bet here would be to start reading up on estrogens which are the main hormone for feminization.
