Anthropometric Measurements

This is a more recent counterpart of https://anthro.cs.uni-freiburg.de/, which, although it has a large amount of data across male and female sexes, it's rather outdated (the data were recorded in 1988) and it lacks mobile support.

Hence the development of this tool that uses less outdated data (ANSUR2) and has mobile support. It has data from 1987 women and 4083 men.
